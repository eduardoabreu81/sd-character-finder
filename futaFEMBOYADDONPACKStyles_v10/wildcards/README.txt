HOW TO USE:

1. Copy all .txt files to your Auto1111/Forge wildcards folder:
   stable-diffusion-webui/extensions/sd-dynamic-prompts/wildcards/

2. In your prompt, use:
   __category__ for random selection from that category

EXAMPLE:
   __base__, __body__, __theme__, __state__, __action__
